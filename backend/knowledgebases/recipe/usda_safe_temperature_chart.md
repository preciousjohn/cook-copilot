## Beef, Pork, Veal and Lamb Steaks, Chops and Roasts

Minimum internal temperature: 145 °F (62.8 °C)  
Rest time: at least 3 minutes

---

## Ground Meats

Minimum internal temperature: 160 °F (71.1 °C)  
Rest time: none required

---

## Ground Poultry

Minimum internal temperature: 165 °F  
Rest time: none required

---

## Ham, Fresh or Smoked (Uncooked)

Minimum internal temperature: 145 °F (62.8 °C)  
Rest time: at least 3 minutes

---

## Fully Cooked Ham (to Reheat)

Minimum internal temperature:
- USDA-inspected plant packaged: 140 °F (60 °C)
- All others: 165 °F (73.9 °C)

Rest time: none required

---

## All Poultry (Breasts, Whole Bird, Legs, Thighs, Wings, Ground Poultry, Giblets and Stuffing)

Minimum internal temperature: 165 °F (73.9 °C)  
Rest time: none required

---

## Eggs

Minimum internal temperature: 160 °F (71.1 °C)  
Rest time: none required

---

## Fish and Shellfish

Minimum internal temperature: 145 °F (62.8 °C)  
Rest time: none required

---

## Leftovers

Minimum internal temperature: 165 °F (73.9 °C)  
Rest time: none required

---

## Casseroles

Minimum internal temperature: 165 °F (73.9 °C)  
Rest time: none required
